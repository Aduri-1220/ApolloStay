# Product Strategy

## Positioning

PulsePilot should not compete as just another calorie tracker. It should compete as an adaptive health operating system for everyday users who want clear guidance, fast logging, and sustained motivation.

## MVP differentiators

- Personalized AI coaching tone with explainable recommendations
- Food database designed for Indian meals plus international foods
- Workout and recovery planning in the same experience
- Premium visual design that feels more like a coach than a spreadsheet

## Ideal feature ladder

1. Free: logging, hydration, basic workouts, trends
2. Premium: AI coach, meal plans, adaptive targets, deeper insights
3. High-ticket: human coach check-ins, lab review, habit programs

## Monetization

- Monthly and annual subscriptions
- In-app coach consultations
- Corporate wellness bundles
- Partnered supplement and healthy meal affiliate flows

## Retention loops

- Daily score
- Weekly insight narrative
- Smart reminders based on habit slippage
- Progressive plans that update as the user improves
